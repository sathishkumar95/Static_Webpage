Industrial & Warehouse Cleaning

High-tech manufacturing and assembly facilities require meticulous care in cleaning and maintenance. GGFM industrial and warehouse specialists have been specifically trained and equipped to provide cleaning and maintenance services that meet and well exceed industry standards.

Marble Floor Crystallisation & Vitrification

Vetrification is the process applied to the surface of natural calcareous flooring such as terrazzo or conglomerate marble. With the right combination of chemicals and application wearing and slip resistant surface, which invariably reduces maintenance and daily cleaning and maintenance. GGFM Vetrification will immensely improve the appearance of marble floors thus creating a flawless spotless finish.
