Incorporated in 2014...

Green Global Facilities Management (GGFM), is an up and coming major player in Facility Management Services sector in the U.A.E. GGFM has the ability to undertake challenging projects in Soft Services, continuous training will be provided to ensure that all the stringent requirements of various clientals are met. With a philosophy of continuous quality improvement together with customer focus, GGFM, is a force to be reckoned with. GGFM is able to provide a whole bouche of soft services in the Facilities Management and Environmental care. GGFM is equipped with the latest heavy and light machinery and tools of its own fleet of vehicles and a workforce of over 300 dedicated professionals. Our scope of expertise lies in but not limited to, Shopping Complexes, Educational Institutions, Government offices, public area, private buildings, Factories, shopping malls, stand alone retail outlets shops, oil & Gas industries, amusement parks, educational, Airports, Hotels, Hospitals, sporting facilities, initial cleaning of newly constructed buildings in and out, all stand to benefit from GGFM competent experienced team.
